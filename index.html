This is a site about my dog, Vega

Who is Vega?

Vega was born 12/27/2014, and she is named after the brightest star in the sky. She is a mix between a maltese, yorkie, and poodle, also known formally as a Morkie-Poo. Sometimes she has "Yorkie brain", which means she loses concentration and cannot focus on anything. Other times she demonstrates an ability to learn new tricks that rivals some humans.

Vega's best features

  • Cutest dog in the world
  • Very loving and greets you with kisses when you wake up
  • Extremely playful
  • Loves other dogs
  • Learns new tricks with grace and aplomb
